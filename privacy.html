Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2025

1. Introduction

CandyWorld ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as:

  • Account registration details (email, username)
  • Conversation content with AI companions
  • Profile preferences and customization settings
  • Payment information (processed via secure third-party providers)

We also collect certain technical data automatically, including IP address, browser type, device type, and usage patterns, to improve service performance and security.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and personalize the CandyWorld service
  • Improve AI companion responses and features
  • Send service updates and security notices
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Security

All conversation data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. We employ regular security audits and penetration testing to protect your data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your conversation history for as long as your account is active. You may delete specific conversations or your entire account at any time from your account settings page.

6. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for research purposes. Service providers (e.g., payment processors, hosting) may access data strictly to perform their services under confidentiality agreements.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. Contact us at privacy@candyworld.ai to exercise these rights.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. Optional analytics cookies help us understand how users interact with the service. You may opt out of analytics cookies at any time.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@candyworld.ai.
