Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2025

1. Introduction

At CandyWorld, we treat your privacy with the highest level of respect and security. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you interact with our AI companions and platform features.

2. Data Collection

We only collect data that is essential for providing a personalized experience:

  • Account Information: Email address and username for synchronization across devices.
  • Conversation Logs: Encrypted logs of your chats to enable the companion memory feature.
  • Technical Data: IP address and device information for security and performance optimization.

3. Data Usage

Your data is used specifically to:

  • Provide and improve our AI companion services.
  • Develop new features and character personas.
  • Ensure the security and integrity of the platform.

4. Encryption & Security

All conversations are end-to-end encrypted. We do not sell your personal data or conversation logs to third-party advertisers or data brokers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request a copy of your data, or request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated conversation logs at any time via your account settings.
